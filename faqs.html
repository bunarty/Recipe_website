I'm just getting into baking. Where do I start ?

Find recipes that excite you study the instructions and start baking! There are many good recipe for beginners on our website, on the recipes page. It is important to find a recipe that you enjoy and then make it!. Look for opportunities to bake. With each time you bake, you learn something new and get a little bit better.

Decorative ideas - How do I prevent tall cakes from leaning ?

The best way to prevent leaning cakes is to level your cakes so each layer is totally flat. Get eye level with each layer you stack and frost to make sure it’s level.

What are the biggest beginner mistakes when baking

One of the biggest mistakes beginner bakers make is not reading the recipe and instructions thoroughly. Make sure you have all the ingredients you need and aren’t trying to substitute anything last minute. Also make sure you’ve read through the instructions ahead of time so you know how long everything takes to make. Rushing through the baking and decorating process makes the whole experience much more stressful. Plan ahead! You can even prep ahead of time making it easier to start at the required time.

Allergy Information

It is important to read through the recipes. It is adviceable to assume that the recipe contains gluten, dairy unless specified.Feel free to tweak recipes to your dietary requirements
